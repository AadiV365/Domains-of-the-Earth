Domains of the Earth

"That's one small step for a man, one giant leap for mankind."

Atmosphere

The atmosphere contains all of the planet's air.

Hydrosphere

The hydrosphere contains all the water of the planet, in the form of oceans, lakes and other boides of water, and water vapour.

Lithosphere

The lithosphere contains all of the hard, solid rock of the planet's surface, in the form of continental lands and ocean basins.

Biosphere

The biosphere contains all of the planet's living organisms.
