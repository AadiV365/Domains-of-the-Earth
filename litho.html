Lithosphere

The solid portion of the Earth is called the Lithosphere

It is made up of the rocks of the Earth's surface and a thin layer of soil that contains nutrients which may feed many living organisms.

The highest point of the Earth's lithosphere is the peak of Mount Everest.
The lowest point of the Earth's surface is the bottom of the Marianas trench.
There are 7 continents, which is a massive area of land which is surrounded by the oceans or natural features.
